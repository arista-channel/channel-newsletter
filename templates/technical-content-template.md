## Usage Guidelines

### Section Descriptions

**Overview**
- Keep to 2-3 sentences maximum
- Lead with business value, not just technical specs
- Answer: What is it? Why does it matter? Who benefits?

**Key Technical Highlights**
- 3-5 bullet points maximum
- Each highlight should be a bold title followed by brief description
- Focus on differentiators and unique capabilities

**Platform Specifications**
- Use when covering specific hardware/software products
- Include multiple variants if applicable
- Keep specs concise and relevant
- Always include "Use Cases" for each platform variant

**Technical Benefits**
- Group into 3-5 major categories
- Each category should have 3-4 sub-benefits
- Balance technical depth with business value
- Use bold formatting for sub-benefit titles

**Deployment Use Cases**
- Include 4-6 real-world scenarios
- Follow Challenge → Solution → Benefits structure
- Make challenges relatable to customer pain points
- Quantify benefits where possible

**Partner Opportunities**
- 3-5 specific business opportunities
- Focus on revenue potential and market expansion
- Tie to partner capabilities and verticals

**Resources**
- Include 3-5 relevant links
- Use target="_blank" for external links
- Mark future resources as "[Coming Soon]"
- Prioritize: Product pages, datasheets, white papers, guides

### Writing Style

- **Technical but Accessible**: Write for engineers who may not be domain experts
- **Action-Oriented**: Focus on what customers can do with the technology
- **Specific**: Use concrete examples, numbers, and specifications
- **Consistent**: Follow the template structure for all articles
- **Scannable**: Use bold formatting, bullet points, and clear headings

### Content Length

- **Total Article**: 800-1200 words
- **Overview**: 50-75 words
- **Each Use Case**: 40-60 words
- **Each Technical Benefit**: 30-50 words per sub-benefit

### Formatting Standards

- Use **bold** for emphasis on key terms and titles
- Use `code formatting` for technical commands, APIs, or protocols
- Use numbered emojis (1️⃣, 2️⃣, 3️⃣) for article numbering
- Use ✍️ emoji before author attribution
- Use consistent spacing (blank line before/after major sections)

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] Author attribution is complete and accurate
- [ ] Overview is concise (2-3 sentences)
- [ ] All technical claims are verified
- [ ] Use cases are realistic and relatable
- [ ] Partner opportunities are specific and actionable
- [ ] All links are tested and use target="_blank"
- [ ] Formatting is consistent with template
- [ ] Content is 800-1200 words
- [ ] Technical depth is appropriate for audience
- [ ] Business value is clearly articulated
